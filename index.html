ABOUT US

LASER POWER & INFRA PVT LTD. is the largest Cable and Conductor Manufacturer & Exporter in Eastern India, with a strong foothold in EPC projects pan-India.

Revolutionising the Power Transmission & Distribution sector for over 3 decades. LPIPL making our nation proud with large-scale electrification projects like 'Har Ghar Yojana' and 'Saubhagya Yojana' becoming a reality because of pioneering engineering works and our wide range of Wires, Cables & Conductors.
