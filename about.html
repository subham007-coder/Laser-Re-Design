About Us

Laser Power & Infra Pvt Ltd (LPIPL) is one of the Largest Cable and Conductor Manufacturers & Exporters in India

Our journey started 50 years back from an electric shop on Ezra Street, Kolkata, India. Today, we are synonymous with India's power resurgence, manufacturing world-class Power Transmission & Distribution products from our state-of-the-art Dhulagarh Facility.

LPIPL develops and markets a wide range of Wires, Cables & Conductors for Domestic and International Markets with proven expertise in large-scale Electrification Projects across the country under prestigious Government-led social initiatives.

LPIPL also manufactures and deploys Medium Voltage (MV) and Low Voltage (LV) Power Cables, Aerial Bunched Cables, Conductors, Control Cables, Instrumentation Cables, Aluminium Rods, Aluminium Alloy Rods and Armour Rods, serving both retail and institutional segments under the brand name "LASER".

To strengthen the Supply Chain, LASER believes in expanding to new verticals through backward integration for Wires, Cables, EPC and Infrastructure Projects like GI Wires, PVC & XLPE compounding, Valves and Water Distributions & Sewerage Systems.

Boasting of a strong foothold in pan-India EPC projects, our clientele includes reputed Central & State Government Power Distribution houses. We are also a preferred supplier to major Power Discoms in India and globally.

We also take pride in being one of the biggest suppliers of Signalling, Control, Quad and Power Cables to the Indian Railways.

Driven by a strong ambition to be the universal brand of choice for consumers across the spectrum of our business segments and beyond boundaries, we have been continuously establishing new touchpoints to connect with them. Our journey shows a continued and committed effort toward building a brand named "LASER" that lives its customers' ethos and delivers to their aspirations. Let's take a look at our journey.

BRAND JOURNEY

At Laser Power Infra, we believe in building a world that is both safer and smarter by ensuring Laser power’s promise of connection and protection to build a better tomorrow. Our journey is filled with memories of learning, achievements and success, crossing innumerable milestones and many more to come.

1988

  • Founding of Laser Power & Infra

    Founded by Shri P.D. Goel in 1988, Laser Power & Infra has pioneered the development of India’s electrical industry & setup Conductor Unit at 20,000 sq. ft. Shed Areas.

  • Launch of Aluminium Wire Plant

    1998 – Setup Cable Unit and shifted to 90,000 sq. ft. Shed Areas. | 2004 – Setup Properzi Aluminium Wire Rode Plant.

2007

  • Major Manufacturing Expansion

    2007 – Started Rural Electrification EPC Projects | 2008 – Shifted Manufacturing Unit at 185,000 sq. ft. Shed Areas.

  • Power Grid & RDSO Approvals

    2009 – Obtained Power Grid & RDSO approvals | 2012 – Entered into Export Market.

  • Venture into Rooftop Solar

    2016 – Acquired further 600,000 sq. ft. area in Mfg Unit | 2017 – Entered into Rooftop Solar Projects.

2018

  • Launch of HT Cable Production

    2018 – Started producing HT Cables up to 33 KV with a State-Of-The-Art CCV Line. | 2021 – Entered into Water Projects.

  • Entry into Gas Distribution

    2022 – Entered into Gas Distribution Projects | Turnover – First 500 G. happened in 2018 & 1400 G. in 2020.

MISSION

Laser Power & Infra Pvt. Ltd aims to make India power-dependent in the next 5 years. We are leading with sustainability and conservation of resources with emphasis on Wind, Solar and Renewable Energy. We have already increased investment in manpower and equipment to confront every obstacle and utilise our full potential from the start of 2021.

At LPIPL, we value growth through evolution. Our research team is constantly improving to venture into new verticals and categories. Our focus is to consistently allocate financial resources and time towards making social responsibility more personal.

VISION

At Laser Power & Infra Pvt. Ltd, our vision is to truly imbibe the 'Make in India' spirit and cater to global demand. By combining our resources, we want to light up every home in our nation. We want to expand our geography and foray into new markets. LPIPL is moving towards being a carbon neutral organisation. By focusing on harnessing renewable energy, we are working for a green and safe planet. In this journey, we envision making technology a way of life at LPIPL, aiming at 100% automation under human supervision.
